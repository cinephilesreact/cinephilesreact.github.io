However, it is still tentative and may change depending on what movies are more interesting to evaluate in the future.

    Reaction - comments, feelings, and look while watching the film (Duration: 6-10 minutes)


    Review - overall feedback and their favorite part, after watching the film (10 - 12 minutes)
